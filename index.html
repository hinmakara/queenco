Welcome to Queenco hotel & casino

Queenco Hotel & Casino is located amidst the blue water and soft sands of Victory Beach. It’s only 17 km away from Sihanoukville International Airport (KOS).

The complex consists of 2 buildings:

  • A beachfront building in colonial style with private beach houses containing 58 rooms & suites.
  • A newly renovated modern building that boasts 141 rooms and suites with beautiful views.

199 luxuriously appointed rooms and suites that ensure convenience, comfort and privacy for travelers.

Whether for leisure or business, guests can find what they need among 4 types of rooms and 5 types of suites.

Our newly renovated rooms & suites are all equipped with air conditioning, internet, smart TVs, and sofas. Mini-bar and tea making facilities are also available, along with an in-room safe.

ROOMS & SUITES
